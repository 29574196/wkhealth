02. About Us

Best Home Care Center
with Love

WK Health- Social Care Services Ltd was created in December 2021 with the aim of supporting the most vulnerable people in our communities. The company Management have wide range of experience and skills from Nursing, Care and Support work and Social Work. The management, Social Workers and Nurses are Social Work England and NMC Registered respectively. Our company specialises in providing care workers, support workers and health workers to care homes and residential homes. We provide care and support workers for vulnerable individuals in the comfort of their own homes. We provide supported living in different setups for different age groups. We also complete assessments of needs for children and families, elderly, those living with disabilities.
